www.wattwurmdesign.de
a design experiment

wattwurm design is a self initiated project in which I want to experiment with the time for a design. I want to evaluate wether it is visible that a design was created in one day, one month or half a year. In november 2018 the idea was born when I wondered why I always got scared of projects I had to do in my studies, especially finishing or sticking to them. This experiment moght help me to create some techniques to structure my work or to realize how I work most efficient and fun.

The first attempt is to create a design in 24 hours from two random things – a need and an adjective.

Since my studies I have no access to a workshop anymore but I am part of the mdf-Plattenbau collective, which is in the process of building a communual workshop in VorOrt Dessau. Hopefully that project will bring fresh wind to this project.
