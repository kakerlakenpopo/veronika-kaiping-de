Hallo, I'm Vero. A German ceramist and illustrator based in Dessau. I have discovered ceramics in 2021 and I am currently working in our community studio in Wolfgangstraße where we also give workshops.

Other than playing with mud I like tomatoes and to illustrate (@kaip_ink). In 2022 I wrote my first comic and fell in love with the artform.

You can find my art in my online-shop or at local studio openings/markets and follow my journey on Instagram & YouTube

If you have an inquiry about my work, want to collaborate or just have a little chat please send me an email. I look forward to hearing from you :)
