Purpose changes everything
Together with Nadja and Laurent from Generation Purpose we came up with the poem for the Purpose Story. Based on the story I created the illustrations and the character «O» who is the protagonist of this story. The booklet visualizes the development of a person with an inner purpose who is starting to use this purpose as a compass and communication tool in their life.

The Studio
The studio where I create my work is also an open ceramics workshop called Tonstudio run by a few people and myself. It is located in the VorOrt e.V. building in Wolfgangstraße 13 and we are surrounded by a bunch of other creatives, designers and artisans.

Our purpose: Tonstudio is an open community of creatives. We focus on learning from and inspiring each other in an inclusive safe-space while experimenting with ceramics.
