I am a generalist designer based in dessau.

I am currently co-initiating the DESIGN WALZ in Germany and I am part of the mdf-Plattenbau collective that will build a communual workshop in Dessau.

I teach myself 3D modeling at the moment and want to try out fun animations and playing with physics.. Since a few weeks I got into silversmithing and I am continuously learning about front end development while experimenting with programming different websites. I really like the problem-finding and -solving experience of programming combined with the headspace I gain while illustrating and doing manual work while building furniture or crafting jewellery.
